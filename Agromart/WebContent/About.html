ABOUT US

Agriculture is prime sector of importance. More than 50% of people are engaged in agriculture activity. To make agribusiness productive and smooth, it is important that it should be linked to recent technologies. The farmers are facing various issues such related to storage, related to transportation. They even lack proper knowledge related to soil, water and crops. In this scenario, a one-stop center would be beneficial for the farmers so that as many as their problems can be solved. The storage center not only solves the problem of storage but along with that it also solves the issues related to maintenance, transportation, selling and generating revenue from it. Moreover, such storage centers can provide employment to those who remains unemployed due to one or many reasons. Thus, it can be beneficial to reduce seasonal unemployment too. Also, the selling module of such storage center provides services to farmers, wholesalers and vendors, it can solve the problem of uneven price distribution. The soil quality is to be maintained with the proper care. Artificial fertilizer can damage the soil as well as the crops can get infected from it. So, by the use of natural fertilizers we can maintain the fertility of soil. The waste material can be used to create the above-mentioned natural fertilizers. Also, bio-energy can be generated from these wastes.
